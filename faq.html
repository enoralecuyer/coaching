Frequently Asked Questions:


1. What is coaching?

We work together to clarify your short-term and long-term goals. Then, we create a map of practical and simple actions to move you forward. I ask you questions to clarify your beliefs and habits so that you can transform what is blocking you. I provide a safe place without advice or judgment where you grow at your own pace. I also provide customized tools and homework to help you thrive between sessions.


2. How is coaching different from consulting?

A consultant gives expertise advices to solve a problem. They lead the session, analyse the issue, come up with a map and solve the problem. The client remains dependent on the consultant to solve a particular issue.

Coaching is different. You acquire customized skills to know exactly how to transform your beliefs and habits on your own, which benefits all areas of your life - relationships, health, finances..., and lead you to your own independence and success.


3. How long does a session last?

Each session lasts one hour and is held over via Zoom/Skype/Phone or in person in Los Angeles.


4. What should I expect from my first coaching session with you?

Your first coaching will assist you to define with clarity your goals and your expectations for coaching. We will define together the best ways to achieve your short-term and long-term projects. We will then work together on the goal of your choice. Ready to take action? Please send me an email at hey.enora@gmail.com.


5. How can I book my first session with you?

Send me an email with the package of your choice (1 hour, 3 months or 6 months). Then, you will be able to book your coaching session(s) on my calendar and to proceed payment via Paypal/Square or direct transfer.
