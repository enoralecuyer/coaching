Are you ready to connect with your values and fully trust yourself?


During our coaching sessions, you will...

  • Overcome fear of being judged, criticized or rejected.
  • Transform your inner-critic into wisdom.
  • Clarify your goals and create practical and simple steps.
  • Create more time and space for your own self-care.
  • Learn to ask for what you need in a clear and honest way.
  • Get rid of perfectionism and procrastination.

    • How does coaching work?


    1. We clarify your short-term and long-term goals.
    2. We explore your beliefs and habits so that you can transform what is blocking you.
    3. We create a map of practical actions to move you forward.
    4. I let you grow at your own pace without advice or judgment.
    5. I give you customized tools and homework to help you thrive between sessions.
